5 Tips to Keep Your T-Shirts Looking New

1. Spring Key Pieces: Lightweight hoodies, denim jackets, graphic tees, cargo pants, sneakers. Styling Tips: Layer a graphic tee under a lightweight jacket. Opt for pastel or muted tones mixed with neutral basics. Sneakers or chunky trainers work well as your main footwear. Accessory Tip: A dad cap or bucket hat adds a casual street vibe.
2. Summer Key Pieces: Oversized tees, shorts, tank tops, lightweight joggers, low-top sneakers. Styling Tips: Keep it loose and breathable. Go for bright or neutral tones. Pair graphic tees with cargo shorts or biker shorts. Accessory Tip: Sunglasses and minimal chains elevate the look.
3. Fall Key Pieces: Hoodies, flannels, bomber jackets, ripped jeans, layered tees. Styling Tips: Use layering for warmth and style: T-shirt + hoodie + light jacket. Stick to earthy tones: olive, beige, brown, maroon. Sneakers or boots depending on vibe. Accessory Tip: Beanies, caps, or scarves can finish the look.
4. Winter Key Pieces: Puffer jackets, oversized hoodies, thermal layers, cargo pants, boots. Styling Tips: Go for bold layering: thermal tee ‚Üí hoodie ‚Üí puffer jacket. Darker tones often fit the winter aesthetic: black, grey, navy. Chunky boots or sneakers with thick socks complete street style.
